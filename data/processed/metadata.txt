# Community Equity Scores Metadata

**File:** community_equity_scores.csv
**Source:** Derived from streetlight_requests.csv analysis
**Creation Date:** Oct 29, 2025
**Methods:** 
  - Aggregated requests by community
  - Calculated fast_rate (% resolved in 0-1 days)
  - Calculated slow_rate (% taking >14 days) 
  - Derived equity_score composite metric
**Fields:** comm_code, comm_name, total_requests, fast_rate, slow_rate, equity_score
